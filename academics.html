Common Questions

How do I choose my electives? ‚ñº
Elective selection is done after admission. Students are advised to choose subjects based on their career interests and BECE strengths. Our guidance counselors are available during orientation to assist with this crucial decision.
Does the school offer Robotics? ‚ñº
Yes! Accra Academy has one of the best Robotics Clubs in Ghana, frequently winning national championships. It is open to Science and Engineering students who demonstrate aptitude for coding and mechanics.
What is the passing grade? ‚ñº
We uphold high standards. The internal passing grade is 50%, but students are encouraged to aim for excellence (A1/B2) to secure university placement. Remedial classes are available for those who need extra support.
