Our Heritage Since 1931

"The first private academy in the Gold Coast established by indigenous investors, pioneering excellence in education for over 90 years."

The Big Four

Founding Fathers

On July 20, 1931, four visionary educators established Accra Academy in Mantse Agbonaa. Their mission was clear: provide quality secondary education to students who could not afford expensive government schools, creating a legacy of resilience and excellence that continues today.

Our Journey

Through the Decades

1931

The Foundation

Founded on July 20th in Ellen House, James Town with just 19 students and a vision for excellence.

1961

The Great Migration

Moved from James Town to the current spacious campus at Bubuashie, marking a new era of growth.

2003

African Excellence

Ranked 8th Best High School in Africa by the Africa Almanac.

Today

Leading Innovation

Pioneering Robotics, AI Clubs, and modern Sports Infrastructure while maintaining academic excellence.
