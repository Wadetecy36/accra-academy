The Big Four

Founding Years (1931)

Accra Academy was founded on July 20, 1931, by four visionary educators in Mantse Agbonaa. Driven by the desire to provide quality secondary education to students who could not afford expensive government schools.

  • 1 Dr. Kofi George Konuah
  • 2 Gottfried Narku Alema
  • 3 James Akwei Halm-Addo
  • 4 Samuel Neils Awuletey

Through the Decades

1931

The Foundation

Founded on July 20th in Ellen House, James Town with just 19 students.

1961

The Great Migration

Moved from James Town to the current spacious campus at Bubuashie.

2003

African Excellence

Ranked 8th Best High School in Africa by the Africa Almanac.
