Admission Process

1

BECE Results

Admissions are processed via the CSSPS system based on your Basic Education Certificate Examination performance.

2

Placement

Check your school placement online. Ensure you have been officially placed in Accra Academy before proceeding.

3

Prospectus

Download and print the official prospectus and admission forms from this portal or pick them up on campus.

4

Registration

Report to the school administration block on the stipulated date for verification and house allocation.
