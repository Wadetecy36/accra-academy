The Big Four

Founding Years (1931)

Accra Academy was founded on July 20, 1931, by four visionary educators in Mantse Agbonaa.

Driven by the desire to provide quality secondary education to students who could not afford the expensive government schools, they established a legacy of resilience and excellence.

  • 1. Dr. Kofi George Konuah
  • 2. Gottfried Narku Alema
  • 3. James Akwei Halm-Addo
  • 4. Samuel Neils Awuletey
