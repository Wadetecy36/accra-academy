Our Journey

A Timeline of Glory

1931

The Genesis

Ellen House

The vision comes to life. Accra Academy is founded with 19 students in James Town. The motto "Esse Quam Videri" is etched into history.

1950

Recognition

Government Assistance

A pivotal moment. We became a Government-Assisted School, a testament to the high academic standards set by the founders.

1961

The Expansion

Little Legon

The Great Migration to Bubuashie. The expansive, modern campus earned the nickname "Little Legon," setting the stage for sports and academic dominance.

2003

Global Stage

Top 10 in Africa

The Africa Almanac ranked Accra Academy as the 8th Best High School in Africa, cementing its status as a continental giant in education.
