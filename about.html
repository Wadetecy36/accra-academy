Our Heritage

"To be, rather than to seem"

Founding Years (1931–1949)

Accra Academy was founded on July 20, 1931, by four visionary educators in Mantse Agbonaa. Starting with 19 students in Ellen House, the school aimed to provide affordable secondary education to brilliant but financially disadvantaged boys.

Modern Era

From the 1980s to present day, Accra Academy has continued to lead in ICT, Sports, and Academics.
